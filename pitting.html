Precautionary Steps for Nail Pitting

Nail pitting may indicate skin or autoimmune disorders. Manage it with the following care tips.

🧼

Clean & Dry Nails

Maintain proper nail hygiene to prevent infections and keep nails healthy.

💅

Moisturize Regularly

Use nail and hand moisturizers to reduce dryness and flaking.

🌿

Avoid Harsh Chemicals

Minimize contact with irritants in detergents and nail products.

🧑‍⚕️

Seek Diagnosis

Consult a dermatologist if pitting persists or is linked to other skin issues.

🥗

Balanced Nutrition

Eat a nutrient-rich diet to support the structure and growth of nails.

✅ Nail pitting could indicate deeper conditions — don’t ignore recurring symptoms.
