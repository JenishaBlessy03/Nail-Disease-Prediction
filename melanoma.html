Precautionary Steps for Acral Lentiginous Melanoma

Protect yourself from this rare form of skin cancer with the following smart and simple precautions.

🔍

Regular Self-Check

Examine your hands, feet, and under your nails for dark streaks or unusual discoloration.

👩‍⚕️

Early Consultation

See a dermatologist immediately if you notice suspicious spots or skin changes.

🌞

Sunscreen Use

Apply SPF to all exposed skin — including hands, feet, and nail beds — to reduce UV risk.

🚫

Limit UV Exposure

Avoid tanning beds and excessive sunbathing to protect sensitive areas.

🧤

Hand & Foot Care

Wear gloves when handling chemicals and moisturize regularly to prevent skin damage.

✅ Taking precautions early can make a life-saving difference. Stay informed, stay safe.
