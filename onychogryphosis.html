Precautionary Steps for Onychogryphosis

Onychogryphosis, or ram's horn nails, causes nails to thicken and curve. These precautions help manage and prevent the condition.

🦵

Regular Trimming

Keep nails trimmed frequently to prevent abnormal thickening and distortion.

🩺

Podiatrist Care

Visit a foot specialist for safe trimming and monitoring of nail growth.

🧼

Foot Hygiene

Wash and dry feet properly to avoid fungal and bacterial infections.

👞

Comfortable Footwear

Wear well-fitting shoes to reduce pressure and trauma on toenails.

🥗

Balanced Diet

Include essential nutrients and vitamins that support healthy nail growth.

✅ Early attention and proper care can prevent complications. Don’t ignore changes in your nails.
